About

Information About the group

  • 2015

    Beginnings

    A survival/reality show called Sixten where 16 contestents participated and only 9 members out of the 16 were chosen to form the girl group as we know today is Twice.

  • October 2015

    First Debut

    The group finally debuted on 20 October 2015 with the release of their album The Story begins title track being Like OOH-AHH.

  • April 2016

    Second Debut

    With the successful debut of their first album, they've release their second album Page Two title track being Cheer Up. This is where the group finally got their 1st music show win with their success on the song.

  • 2016 - Present

    Moving On

    After their first music show win, TWICE has evolved as a group and has grown locally and internationally as a KPOP group. With their recent comeback, the album has reached the billboard charts Top 10 and this shows that TWICE has really grown. Fans will be anticipating for their next comeback.

Im Na-yeon (임나연)

.

Im Na-yeon (Hangul: 임나연, Japanese: イム ナヨン), better known by her stage name, Nayeon (Hangul: 나연, Japanese: ナヨン) is a South Korean singer. She is the oldest member, lead vocalist and face of the girl group Twice.

  • Stage Name: Nayeon
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Date Of Birth: September 22, 1995

Yoo Jeong-yeon (유정연)

.

HYoo Jeong-yeon (Hangul: 유정연, originally named Yoo Kyung-wan) better known by her first name, Jeongyeon (Hangul: 정연), is a South Korean singer. She is a member of the girl group, Twice, as a lead vocalist of the group.

  • Stage Name: Jeongyeon
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Date Of Birth: November 1, 1996

Hirai Momo (平井もも)

.

Momo Hirai (Hangul: 히라이 모모, Japanese: 平井もも／ひらいもも Hirai Momo), better known as Momo (Hangul: 모모), is a Japanese singer and dancer, active in South Korea. She is the third oldest member of the K-pop girl group, Twice, as the main dancer, vocalist, and sub-rapper.

  • Stage Name: Momo
  • Nationality: Japanese
  • Date Of Birth: November 9, 1996

Minatozaki Sana (サナ)

.

Sana Minatozaki (Hangul: 미나토자키 사나, Japanese: 湊崎紗夏 / みなとざきさな), better known as Sana (Hangul: 사나), is a Japanese singer. She is a member of Twice as a vocalist of the group.

  • Stage Name: Sana
  • Nationality: Japanese
  • Date Of Birth: December 29, 1996

Park Ji-hyo (박지효)

.

Park Ji-hyo ( Hangul: 박지효, born as Park Ji-soo), better known by her first name, Jihyo (Hangul: 지효), is a South Korean singer and dancer, and is the leader and main vocalist of Twice.

  • Stage Name: Jihyo
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Date Of Birth: February 1, 1997

Myoui Mina (名井 南)

.

Mina Myoui (Hangul: 묘이 미나, Japanese: 名井南 / みょういみな), known mostly as Mina (Hangul: 미나), is a Japanese singer. She is a member of Twice as a sub-vocalist and the main dancer.

  • Stage Name: Mina
  • Nationality: Japanese
  • Date Of Birth: March 24, 1997

Kim Da-Hyun (김다현)

.

Kim Da-hyun (Hangul: 김다현), better known by her first name, Dahyun (Hangul: 다현), is a South Korean singer and rapper. She is a vocalist and the lead rapper of the group.

  • Stage Name: Dahyun
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Date Of Birth: May 28, 1998

Son Chae-young (손채영)

.

Son Chae-young (Hangul: 손채영), better known by her first name Chaeyoung (Hangul: 채영), is a South Korean singer, rapper, songwriter and dancer. A member of the girl group Twice, she was assigned the role of the group's main rapper.

  • Stage Name: Chaeyoung
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Date Of Birth: April 23, 1999

Chou Zi-yu (周子瑜)

.

Chou Tzuyu (Hangul: 주쯔위, Chinese: 周子瑜), best known by her stage name, Tzuyu (Hangul: 쯔위), is a Taiwanese singer and dancer, currently based in South Korea. She is a member of Twice, as a lead dancer, sub-vocalist, visual, and the maknae.

  • Stage Name: Tzuyu
  • Nationality: Taiwanese
  • Date Of Birth: June 14, 1999
