Jeon Hee-Jin (전희진)

.

HeeJin (Hangul: 희진) is the first revealed member of LOONA and a member of its first sub-unit, LOONA 1/3. She was born as Jeon Hee-Jin (Hangul: 전희진) on October 19, 2000 in Bundang District, Seongnam, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea. She debuted on September 26, 2016 as a member and subsequently released her solo single album HeeJin on October 5, 2016.

  • Stage Name: Heejin
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Date Of Birth: October 19, 2000
  • Color: Bright Pink

Kim Hyun-Jin (김현진)

.

HyunJin (Hangul: 현진) is the second revealed member of LOONA and a member of its first sub-unit, LOONA 1/3. She was born as Kim Hyun-Jin (Hangul: 김현진) on November 15, 2000 in Sillim, Gwanak District, Seoul, South Korea. She debuted on October 28, 2016 as a member of LOONA and subsequently released her solo single album HyunJin on November 17, 2016.

  • Stage Name: Hyunjin
  • Nationality: Korean
  • Date Of Birth: November 15, 2000
  • Color: Yellow

Cho Ha-Seul (조하슬)

.

HaSeul (Hangul: 하슬) is the third revealed member of LOONA and a member of its first sub-unit, LOONA 1/3. She was born as Cho Ha-Seul[3] (Hangul: 조하슬) on August 18, 1997, in Suncheon, South Jeolla Province, South Korea.[4] She debuted on December 8, 2016 as a member and subsequently released her solo single album HaSeul on December 15, 2016.

  • Date Of Birth: August 18, 1997
